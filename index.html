Hi.

I'm a second year PhD student studying Computer Science at University of Chicago. I did my undergraduate at Princeton University, and am originally from Ottawa, Canada (eh)!

My research interests are in databases, data systems, and machine learning. My advisor is Sanjay Krishnan, and I'm a part of the ChiData Group . I am currently working on the ChiVideo project, IoT analytics, and information leakage.

Corresponding to these projects, there are two specific research questions I'm currently interested in. (1) How do we create multimedia databases and systems (particularly with video) that support general computer vision analytics? (2) How do we enforce the barriers in machine learning pipelines that provide generality guarantees?
